You can download KeyboardKit directly from the App Store.

About

📱

The KeyboardKit app lets you create custom keyboards directly on your iPhone and iPad.

These keyboards support languages and autocomplete, as well as audio & haptic feedback.

You can also create themes that customize the look of your keyboards.

Themes

🎨

The KeyboardKit app lets you create themes that can customize the look of your keyboards.

A theme defines things like background and button colors, borders, shadows etc.

Languages

KeyboardKit supports creating keyboards with the following languages:

  • 🇺🇸 English (US - Default)
  • 🇩🇰 Danish
  • 🇳🇱 Dutch
  • 🇬🇧 English (UK)
  • 🇫🇮 Finnish
  • 🇫🇷 French
  • 🇩🇪 German
  • 🇮🇹 Italian
  • 🇳🇴 Norwegian
  • 🇪🇸 Spanish
  • 🇸🇪 Swedish

More languages are planned. Please reach out if you have suggestions.

Sharing

🎁

You can share your keyboards and themes with anyone who has the app.

You can also publish your creations for a chance to get them features in the app.
